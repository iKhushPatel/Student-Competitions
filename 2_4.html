About SRMJEEE 2016

SRMJEEE 2016 will be conducted by SRM University for admission to its various undergraduate engineering programme in SRM 

Engineering College.
SRMJEEE 2016 application process has begun in October 19, 2015. 
SRMJEEE 2016 computer based exam would be conducted between April 19 to April 25, 2016. SRM has done away with Pen and 

Paper based exam from this year onwards.
SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination (SRMJEEE) for B.Tech is common for SRM Group of Universities viz., SRM 

University and SRM University HARYANA.
About SRM University

The SRM University (Sri Ramaswamy Memorial University), formerly SRM Institute of Science and Technology, is a co-

educational private university in the state of Tamil Nadu, India.
