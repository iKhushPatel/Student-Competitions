About WBJEE 2016 Medical for Medical & Pharmacy

WBJEE is a common entrance exam for admission to Universities, Govt. Colleges and Self-financed Institutes of West Bengal 

to Undergraduate courses in Medical, Dental, Engineering & Technology, Pharmacy and Architecture.
WBJEE 2016 will be held for admission to various Universities/Colleges/ self financing technical institutes in West Bengal 

for degree in Medical, Dental,Engineering, Architecture & Pharmacy and Architecture.
WBJEEM can also be taken by students from outside West Bengal for some of the courses.
This page is specifically about WBJEE Medical 2016 Exam

WBJEE Medical exam is held yearly for admission to various Medical Colleges/Universitiesin West Bengal.
