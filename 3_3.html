Overview of MBBS Course offered

The MBBS course is offered at KMC, Manipal and KMC, Mangalore.
Duration: four and a half years plus one year of compulsory rotating internship course.
This is recognised by the General Medical Council of Great Britain and the Malaysian Medical Council.
The course conducts theory classes and clinical training according to MCI guidelines.

Overview of the BDS Course Offered

The BDS course is offered at MMMC and MCODS Manipal and MCODS Mangalore.
Duration: 4-year course and 1 year internship
Formative and summative assessments are carried out.

Overview of the BPharm (Practice ) Course Offered

The BPharm (Practice ) is offered at Manipal College of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Manipal 
Duration: 2 years of academic program plus one year of internship

Overview of the BPharm - Bachelor of Phamacy Course Offered

The BPharm - Bachelor of Phamacy is offered at Manipal College of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Manipal 
Duration: 4 years
