About WEJEEE 2016


WBJEE is a common entrance exam for admission to Universities, Govt. Colleges and Self-financed Institutes of West Bengal 
into Undergraduate courses in Medical, Dental, Engineering & Technology, Pharmacy and Architecture.
WBJEE 2016 will be held for admission to various Universities /Colleges / self financing technical institutes in West 
Bengal for degree in Medical, Dental,Engineering, Architecture & Pharmacy and Architecture.
