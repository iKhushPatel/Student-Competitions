About KEAM 2016 - CEE Kerala 

KEAM 2016, Kerala Engineering and Medical Entrance Examination 2016 is conducted by The Commissioner for Entrance 
Examinations (CEE), Kerala Government.
KEAM 2016 is conducted for admission into Medical Courses, Agriculture, Veterinary, Fisheries, Engineering & Architecture 
Course in the state of Kerala for the academic session 2016-2017
KEAM is the major entrance exam in which near about one lakh twenty five thousand candidates participate every year.
One one application is to be filled for admission to all of these courses.
